CrosswordChat voice commands

Everything you can say once a session is listening.

Start a session from the extension icon or the in-page button, then just speak. Say your answer as a whole word, or spell it out. When the recognizer mishears you, the phrases below steer it — most have a few wordings, and any of them work. You never have to remember the exact one.

Answering

Say the answer
Just say the word — heart. Multi-word answers work too: a lot.
Spell it out
Say the letters — H, E, A, R, T — as bare letters, letter names (aitch, ee, ay…), or the NATO alphabet (hotel, echo, alpha…).
Say it, then spell it
Give the word and its spelling in one breath: dog, D, O, G.
Fill just the blanks
On a half-filled entry, say only the missing letters and the grid supplies the rest.
Force an answer
answer… · guess… · the answer is… · the word is… · try…
Enter a word even if it sounds like a command — answer pass enters PASS.
Enter over a clash
anyway · say it anyway · overwrite · force it
Enter a word that disagrees with letters already in the grid.
That's not what I said
you misheard · that's not what I said · I said… · I meant…
Reject the last guess and try again; I said plane re-answers directly.

Getting around

Next clue
next · next clue · pass · skip · skip it · move on
Leave the current entry as-is and move to the next one.
Previous clue
back · go back · previous · previous clue
Return to the clue you were just on.
Cross the current letter
flip · flip it · switch direction · other direction
Jump to the clue crossing the selected square.
Go to a clue
seven across · 22 down · go to twelve across
Jump straight to any clue by its number and direction. Starting with go to… forces navigation even if the tail is misheard.
Repeat the clue
repeat · say again · again · read it again · what
Read the current clue aloud again.
Change the order
switch to most filled · go in order
Switch how next chooses clues — list order, or the most-filled first.

Fixing the grid

Undo
undo · undo that · take it back
Revert the last answer the session entered.
Clear the entry
clear · clear it · delete · erase
Empty every letter of the current entry. Undo brings it back.
Pencil mode
pencil · pencil mode · use pencil
Write following answers as pencil marks (uncertain).
Pen mode
pen · pen mode · ink
Go back to writing answers in pen.

Spelling & hints

Spell a word
spell · let me spell it · spell A B C
Enter spelling mode, letter by letter. The letters can ride along: spell A B C. While spelling, say done to finish, undo to drop the last letter, or cancel to back out.
Hear the letters
hint · letters · what do I have · what's filled in · spell it
Read back the letters filled in so far, and how many of the entry remain.
List the commands
help · what can I say · commands
Speak a quick spoken summary of the core commands.

Ending the session

Stop
stop · goodbye · bye · quit · we're done · I'm done
End the session with a short sign-off. You can say it even while it's talking.
Other ways to stop
Press Esc, click the in-page button, or click the extension icon — each ends the session instantly and silently. After about a minute of silence the mic closes on its own.
